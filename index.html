AR Experience Requires Permissions

To play AR Tetris, we need access to your device's camera and motion sensors for the augmented reality experience.

  • 1. Camera Access: Allow camera permission when prompted
  • 2. AR Mode: After starting the game, look for the AR icon ( ) in your browser's address bar
  • 3. Enable AR: Tap the AR icon and select "Augmented Reality" mode
  • 4. Floor Scanning: Tilt your device downward to scan the floor

Make sure you're in a well-lit area with a flat surface for best results.
